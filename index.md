# Privacy Policy for Vigor
**Last updated: May 3, 2026**

## Overview
Vigor is a free, offline workout tracking application. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use Vigor.

## Data Collection
Vigor does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and has no servers, user accounts, or analytics.

## Data Storage
All data you enter in Vigor - including workouts, exercises, measurements, and settings - is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Data Retention and Deletion
Your data is stored on your device for as long as you have the app installed. You can delete your data at any time in the following ways:

- Delete individual workouts, exercises, or measurements directly within the app
- Delete all app data by uninstalling Vigor from your device
- Clear app data by going to your device Settings → Apps → Vigor → Clear Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app or clearing app data permanently removes all data associated with Vigor. The developer has no access to your data and therefore cannot delete it on your behalf.

## Google Drive Backup
Vigor uses Android's automatic backup feature to back up your app data to your personal Google Drive account. This backup:

- Is stored in your own Google account, not accessible to the developer
- Is controlled entirely by you through your Google account settings
- Can be disabled in your device settings under Backup
- Is governed by Google's own privacy policy

To delete backed up data, go to your Google Drive settings and remove the Vigor app backup.

## Permissions
Vigor requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications** - Used to display a persistent notification while a workout is active. This keeps the app running in the background so rest timers and sounds work correctly when your phone is locked or the app is minimized. This notification does not collect or transmit any data.

Vigor does not request access to camera, microphone, location, or contacts.

## Third Party Services
Vigor uses Google Play Billing for optional in-app tips. These transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's privacy policy. The developer receives no personal information from these transactions beyond the payment itself.

## Children's Privacy
Vigor does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
